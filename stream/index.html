The Planets ?

Each attendee has their own Planet which accumulates debris based on the books they've checked out from our Library. This displays the most recently changed Planet.

The Orrery ?

The Orrery models how the checkouts of attendees interact. Each point is an attendee's Planet; similar planets aggregate together.

The Galaxy ?

The Galaxy cycles through questions extracted from books attendees have checked out.
